The story

Born in the
heart of the wild.

Deep in South Africa's Karoo, on the banks of the Orange River, farmlands have long been overtaken by thousands of invasive mesquite thorn trees. What most saw as a nuisance, one family saw as an opportunity.

Through 26 years working with mesquite, brothers Aam and Brandt Coetzee discovered these golden seedpods could be slow-roasted into something remarkable: a rich, smooth, naturally sweet espresso with none of the downsides of coffee - and all of the ritual.

How it gets to you.

A small annual window. A meticulous process. Zero shortcuts.

1
Harvest

During a 2 to 3 week window each year, over a thousand locals hand-collect mesquite seedpods in Calvinia, Northern Cape, paid by weight.

2
Sort

Pods are hand-sorted twice: once in the field, once at the processing plant in Somerset West. Only the finest pods make the cut.

3
Roast

Slow-roasting draws out the natural caramel sweetness and nutty depth of the mesquite pod. No artificial flavours are ever added.

4
Brew

Pre-ground to the ideal consistency, Mesqo works in any coffee maker. No new equipment, no grinder, no workflow changes needed.

Why Mesqo

Good for you.
Genuinely.

Not another decaf with the same problems dressed differently. Mesqo is a different category entirely - a roasted mesquite espresso that happens to be naturally caffeine-free.

Zero caffeine, smooth lift
Natural sugars in the mesquite pod give a gentle sense of uplift without the crash, the jitters, or keeping you awake at night.
High fibre, low glycaemic
Unlike coffee or decaf, mesquite is high in dietary fibre and has a low glycaemic index - meaning it supports steadier blood sugar rather than spiking it.
Mineral-rich ingredient
Packed with calcium, magnesium, potassium, zinc, and iron. Mesqo brings genuine nutritional value without turning into a wellness cliché.
No acidity, easier on digestion
Coffee's acidity affects millions. Mesqo has none. It is gentler on the stomach and digestive system - suitable for people who have given up on coffee entirely.
Safe for everyone
No caffeine means Mesqo is suitable during pregnancy, for nursing mothers, for children, and for anyone managing anxiety, heart conditions, or sleep issues.

The people behind every cup

Every harvest
changes a family.

In Calvinia, Northern Cape, unemployment sits at 40%. Each year, a small harvesting window opens - and over a thousand people walk into the fields at dawn to collect pods by hand, paid by weight. For many, it is the most meaningful income of the year.

1,000+
